WORLDS OF ADVENTURE AWAIT YOU!



Currently Online maintains multiple active clans in a variety of different games! Below you’ll find a listing of every CO clan in alphabetical order by game name, as well as a feature listing and contact information for each clan.

Just join us in Discord and follow the instructions to get roles for your favorite games!

 BLOOD, GUTS, AND GLORY ARE JUST BEYOND!

Currently Online’s Diablo clan has been shredding the legions of hell since 2016. Lead by a dedicated team of Diablo addicts, the Diablo clan maintains an active player base, and often even has a waiting list to join! Come experience Diablo in a more social way than ever before.

The Diablo clan has active players throughout each season, massive season launch parties, weekly group play nights, and more. Everyone—from casuals to competitive players—will find a welcoming home and a friendly community to play with in Currently Online!

Clan Features

  • Active players throughout each season

  • Regular social events

  • Pingable LFG roles for world bosses and Nightmare Dungeons

  • Helpful and friendly environment

Discord Contacts

@prrrrrrana - Diablo guildmaster

AN ADVENTURE AWAITS YOU IN EORZEA

Currently Online has an active and social FFXIV free company waiting for you. The Final Fantasy FC was our first clan ever established outside World of Warcraft, and you can expect the same veteran leadership and standard of quality which grew our WoW guild to renown.

The FC maintains active raid teams, hosts regular social events in game such as house parties, and runs PvE group content regularly. Whether you’re simply looking for a place to run your duties with friends, a new team to raid with, or a social environment to call home, our XIV FC has you covered.

Clan Features

  • Active PvE team

  • Regular social events

  • Large fully decorated FC house

  • Active players running group content

Discord Contacts

@Atins - ffxiv Guildmaster

CHASING THE NEXT DOPAMINE HIT

Buying GF is a small, tight knit crew, always looking to add more friends to the mix. Currently we do not have an RS3 clan, though some members of the community do play RS3.  WHERE IT ALL BEGAN

Currently Online traces its roots back to World of Warcraft, and still maintains a strong presence in the game today. At our peak in 2015 Currently Online was the largest WoW guild in the world, and after down-sizing (due to time required to manage that many guilds), we now maintain one Alliance guild on Sargeras, and one Horde guild on Illidan.

Our WoW guilds remain our largest and most active clan to this day! With multiple raid teams on each faction, PvP teams, guild repairs and free bags, and an active community looking for content, you’ll never play alone again. Our raid leaders and guild officers are seasoned veterans to the game and to Currently Online. You can expect a fun, friendly environment, as well as long term stability with us. Looking forward to seeing you all in Azeroth!

Clan Features

  • Very active players and officers

  • Regular PUGs

  • Multiple raid teams

  • Social events

  • Active Keystone groups & pingable LFG roles
